Football / Futsal

FIFA-aligned football pitches and futsal courts designed for consistent ball roll, player safety, and year-round performance.

Dimensions & Standards

Football fieldLength 90–120 m, width 45–90 m
International footballLength 100–110 m, width 64–75 m
Futsal courtLength 25–42 m, width 16–25 m
International futsalLength 38–42 m, width 20–25 m

Surfaces Used

  • Natural grass
  • FIFA Quality artificial turf
  • PU or acrylic futsal flooring

Certifications & Governing Bodies

  • FIFA Quality Programme for Football Turf
  • FIFA Futsal Laws of the Game

Design & Performance Notes

Pitch performance depends on sub-base stability, drainage, and surface resilience. For football, the goal is predictable ball roll and consistent traction across all seasons.

Futsal courts require smooth, high-grip finishes with controlled slip resistance and clear line markings. Lighting and runoff zones should be designed to match competition needs.
